Major Story Arcs

  • Introduction Arc: Yuji Itadori joins Jujutsu High and meets Gojo.
  • Cursed Womb Arc: First major battle against a Special Grade Curse.
  • Kyoto Goodwill Event Arc: Tokyo vs Kyoto students in a battle competition.
  • Shibuya Incident Arc: One of the most intense battles involving Gojo and Geto.

Epic Battles

  • Gojo vs Jogo: Gojo demonstrates his overwhelming power.
  • Yuji & Todo vs Hanami: A high-energy battle showcasing Black Flash.
  • Megumi vs Finger Bearer: Megumi unlocks his full potential.
  • Yuji vs Mahito: A personal and emotional battle of revenge.
